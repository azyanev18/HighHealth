Cardiology



A great part of the patients which need a medical help, suffer from cardiovascular diseases. Veterinary clinic “Blue cross” offers modern diagnostic and therapeutical methods. Here can be diagnosed and cure all cardiac and vascular diseases.

Our doctors constantly upgrade their knowledge by visiting courses, seminars and congresses. Many of our colleagues throughout the country use our facilities and knowledge for precise diagnostics in order to make a more accurate diagnosis. Except regular auscultation and radiofraphs of the heart we do some specific examinations like echocardiography and electrocardiography.

-Echocardiography-we use last generation Doppler ultrasound, for the utmost exact diagnostics. This is the only non-invasive method for examination of the internal structure and function of heart. It gives information for the sizes of the heart parts-ventricles, atria, the thickness and motion of their walls, the form and motion of the valves, for observation of the blood flow in the big vessels, their structure and diameter. Echocardiography is a sensitive tool for diagnosing of pleural and pericardial effusion, cardiac neoplasia or such, adjacent to the heart, for revealing of thrombi in the ventricles and atria. With the help of the echocardiography can be diagnosed different congenital heart diseases of the dog and cat /patent ductus arteriosus, pulmonic stenosis, aortic stenosis, mitral and tricuspid valve dysplasia, atrial septal defect /, as also acquired diseases /valve changes, all cardiomyopathies/.

-Electrocardiographygives us information for the heart rate, rhythm, conductability, dilation and hypertrophy of the ventricles, injury of the myocardium, ischaemia, pericardial diseases, electrolyte disturbances.
