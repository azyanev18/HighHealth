ABOUT US



HighHealth Veterinary clinic is a 24 hour Small Animal Hospital working without a day off. It is built as such starting from the project, then the construction, the equipment and finally to putting it into operation.

It is one of the biggest and modernly equipped veterinary clinics in the US. We use the most up to date equipment available for emergency care, surgery and laboratory activities. On area of more than 400 sq. m., are disposed: waiting room and reception, two consulting rooms, laboratory, x-ray and ultrasound department, operating theatre, intensive care unit.

The clinic has highly qualified team of one of the best doctors in the country, who follow and visit during the whole year courses, seminars and congresses all over the world, in order to be up to date with the newest in veterinary medicine.

We offer a variety of services such as:

  • Emergency care
  • Pharmacy
  • Grooming salon
  • Adoption
  • Boarding
  • Breeding
  • Animal shelters
  • Useful information about your pet
