Dermatology



We carry out large number of diagnostic and therapeutical procedures for all kinds of dermatological diseases:

-Treatment of heavy bacterial infections: with modern antibiotics

-Diagnostics and treatment of fungal diseases, using Wood’s light examination of the skin, culture media, histological evaluation and microscopic observation of preparations.

-Diagnostics and treatment of all kinds of skin parasitic diseases: infestation with fleas, sucking lice, chewing lice, cheyletiellosis. Treatment of refractory and severe generalized demodicosis.

-Diagnostics and treatment of skin allergic diseases, caused by parasites, food, or other allergens.

-Dermatosurgery-operative treatment of benign and malignant skin neoplasia certainly with histological examination, removal of skin masses with cosmetic aim. We are equipped with modern laboratory for correct and sharp diagnostics.
