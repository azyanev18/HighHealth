Pharmacy



Our Pharmacy Technicians

Our trained pharmacy technicians are incredibly knowledgeable and can explain many common (and uncommon) side effects for medications that have been prescribed.

Medicating difficult pets can be a challenge, and our experienced staff regularly take the time to help clients learn how to safely administer medications and encourage pets to become comfortable with the process.

Cost-Saving Measures

Oftentimes, manufacturers of veterinary medications offer rebates with the purchase of pet products. Our pharmacy team tracks this information and is happy to provide it to our clients to pass the savings along. We also conduct online price comparisons to ensure owners are receiving the best deal on medications for their pets.

Over-the-Counter Products

In addition to prescription medications, our pharmacy is stocked with a variety of preventive aids such as flea and tick products, heartworm preventives, an dental health products. Our pharmacy technicians can help you determine the best options based on your pet’s age, breed, and overall health.

Given the many choices in pet nutrition, we also carry a wide selection of prescription diets and high-quality general pet foods.
