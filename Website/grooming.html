Groomin salon



HighHealth Veterinary Clinic offers pet owners:

-Grooming - a collective expression for the overall care of the appearance of the dog, which includes: bathing, combing, drying, cleaning the ears, cutting nails, trimming according to the standard of the breed.

The time of the procedure is determined depending on the size of the breed and the condition of the coat.

-Haircut - it is part of the care that is necessary to maintain the aesthetic and healthy appearance of the dog or cat, helping owners in terms of missed maintenance or relief of the pet with long hair in the warmer months.

It is correct to comb the breeds with long hair at least once a day, and the trimming of the hair in the area of ​​the chin, mouth, ears, paws and abdomen can be done once a month for hygienic purposes after walks and meals.

For certain breeds, the hairstyle is mandatory - a poodle, a schnauzer and most terriers need a haircut every two or three months. Their hairstyles are standard, and the poodles have the greatest variety. For short-haired breeds, it is necessary to treat once or twice a week with suitable brushes in order to brush off the dirt and hair loss.

The weather conditions and the vagaries and specifics of the weather should not be ignored - it would be wrong to shave the skin of our pets that are standing or tied outside, this would lead to overheating of the body or heat stroke.

Regular combing is an integral part of the daily care of the pet and in order to remove dead fibers and improve the body's thermoregulatory function, aeration of the skin and thus reduce the risk of fungal and bacterial infections.

Owners should try to make daily combing a pleasure for both parties, which also plays the role of massage and improves the blood supply to the skin.

Veterinary Clinic HighHealth during haircuts can recommend services and products that will help and relieve you in the daily care of your pet - cutting nails and cleaning ears and eyes with healing lotions ...

Bathing and drying

HighHealth Veterinary Clinic offers bathing of pets with appropriate cosmetics in accordance with the breed characteristics. You will also receive professional advice on proper care for your pet's skin and fur.

Regular bathing is a must for any pet that lives at home. Use only special shampoos for your pet. Human cosmetics are not suitable because of the different pH of the skin that animals have. The clinic also has different types of medicated shampoos, tailored to individual problems and specific requirements of the patient.
